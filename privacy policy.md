# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 26 September 2025

IonoScope (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and stores information when you use the app.

---

## Information We Collect
- **GPS Location Data:**  
  We access your device’s GPS data in order to calculate ionospheric density. This data is required for the app to function properly.

---

## How We Use Information
- GPS data is used **only within the app** to perform ionospheric density calculations.  
- We do **not** collect or transmit your location data to external servers.  
- All GPS data remains **stored locally** on your device.

---

## Data Storage
- Your data is stored **only on your device**.  
- You control if and when your data is shared. Export of data is **manual** and performed solely by you.  
- We do not automatically back up or upload your data.

---

## Data Sharing
- We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.  
- Any sharing of data is entirely at your discretion when you choose to export it.

---

## User Control
- You may stop providing GPS data by disabling location permissions in your device settings. However, this will limit the app’s core functionality.  
- You may delete your stored data at any time by uninstalling the app.

---

## Security
- Since all data is stored locally, it is protected by your device’s operating system security.  
- We do not transfer your data across networks, reducing exposure to external risks.

---

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, we will update the **Effective Date** above.
